Don't waste your time, organize!

Everyday life is demanding and it can take a toll on us. Even though the times have changed (and are changing), women still bear more than their fair share of family and household obligations. How can it be helped? We could wait for a slow and steady change in gender roles and social norms to liberate our time, nevertheless, on a relative scale (such as one's lifetime) those gradual processes usually take significant amount of time. What can be done to feel an immediate, swift and noticable change?

Technology, of course!
